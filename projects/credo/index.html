Credo

Hyderabad, India

Office interiors

4000 sqft

The idea was to create a layout with a fluid concept which felt more organic in nature. Which has enclosures but also has a sense of openness and visual connectivity. The only material which made sense was the usage of curved glass- a contemporary take on the traditional solid partition walls. It also allowed natural light to flow through the space, maximizing the perception of space and preventing the space from feeling enclosed or cramped.

With a strong overlying theme- the wooden lighting, furniture and lounge seating have been carefully designed using organic shapes and soft lines which adds to the fluid concept.

The overall material palette like the use of lime plasters, veneer, green wall and glass also accentuates the concept of fluidity and adds to the organic feel of the space.
