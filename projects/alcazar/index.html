The Alcazar

Bangalore, India

This apartment was our endeavor to create a luxurious yet cozy ambience across all the rooms that are mindfully designed but not ostentatious. The neutral backdrop of the living room is sprinkled with accents of yellow, wooden and metallic elements that altogether exude finesse. A similar concept is adopted for the bedrooms as well, with vibrant hues interspersing the otherwise dominant earthy colors palette. The juxtaposition of floral and geometrical patterns that embellish different surfaces throughout the abode yield sophistication to the spaces. Plush upholstery, well-polished surfaces and splashes of rich colors are characteristic to the interiors of this abode.
